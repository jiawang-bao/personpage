Wireless synchronized pulse transmission system

Jan 1, 2018

In the wireless synchronization pulse transmission system, the transmitter sends the received synchronization pulse to the receiver wirelessly, and the receiver processes it and outputs it. The specific indicators are as follows:

  1. The transmission delay is not greater than 5 microseconds;
  2. The peak transmit power is no more than 1 watt;
  3. The transmission distance is not less than 1 km;
  4. The size of the signal processing board should be as small as possible, and the power supply voltage should be 12V;
  5. The synchronous start pulse detection probability is not less than 99.9%, and the false alarm probability is not more than 1%;
  6. It can realize the work of one master and multiple slaves, and the number of slaves is not less than 8.
