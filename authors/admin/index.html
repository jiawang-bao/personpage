Jiawang Li

PhD Student

Lund University

I loved studying electronic products when I was in high school. I received my bachelor’s degree from Harbin Engineering University in 2019. During this period, I received excellent grades and numerous awards, includin First prize in the Heilongjiang Provincial Electronic Design Competition for three consecutive years, second prize in the National College Student Electronic Design Competition, second prize (top 10 teams in China) in the TI Cup Analog Invitational Competition (sponsored by the famous American semiconductor company Texas Instruments，only one team per school), Ministry of Industry and Information Technology Scholarship (National defense), etc.. I obtained my master’s degree from Southeast University in 2022. During this period, I mainly conducted research on radio frequency systems and antennas. Responsible for the research and development of many projects, including large satellite phased array antennas and circuit systems, new system radar development, ultra-wideband radio detection system, etc. My master’s thesis won the Excellent Paper of the China Electronics Society (Only about 40 per year in China). At the same time, I also won other awards, such as Rohde & Schwarz Scholarship, Chuangyuan Scholarship, First Prize in Graduate Electronic Design Competition, etc. After graduation, I received many offers, including ZTE Blue Sword Plan, TPlink SSSP (Highest salary for fresh Master graduates), Huawei senior engineer (Level 16, the first senior engineer to join the Shanghai CBG department with a master’s degree). In 2020, I interned at Ericsson in Beijing for 3 months. I was mainly responsible for the design and R&D of base station power amplifiers（600M-1GHz）. I successfully completed and implemented the design and development of broadband PA in a limited space. In 2021, I interned at Nanjing Purple Mountain Lab for more than half a year, mainly responsible for satellite phased array system antenna design and RF front-end research and development work. I joined Huawei in 2022 and engage in the development of related millimeter wave radars and terminal mobile phones and won some Root Technology Breakthrough Awards. I joined Lund University in Sweden at the end of 2023 and engage in wireless communication related work.
